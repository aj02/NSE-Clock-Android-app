NSE Clock — beeps every candle, exactly on time.

A dark trading-terminal companion that sounds a distinct alarm at market open (09:00), trading start (09:15), every 5-minute candle close, and market close (15:30 IST). Runs as a persistent background service, re-arms on boot, and fires exact alarms straight through Doze and silent mode.
